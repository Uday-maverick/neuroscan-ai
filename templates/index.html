System Overview

This research prototype classifies structural brain MRI scans into four categories of cognitive impairment severity. The system is designed for research and educational purposes to explore the application of deep learning in neuroimaging analysis.

What this system does: Processes T1-weighted structural MRI images and outputs a predicted classification category with associated confidence scores and model attention visualizations.

What this system does not do: This system does not provide medical diagnosis, is not FDA-approved, and should not be used for clinical decision-making or patient care.
